Powers

Power Effect = Power level + Main Attribute Average.

Power Uses/day = Attribute Average x2

DC out of combat is 0, unless specified.

DC in combat depends on situation and terrain, can be 11-19.

All physical powers are touch, unless specified.

Psychic (Mental)

Level 1:

  • Read Minds
  • Mental Attack (dmg = Power Effect/2 roundup)
  • Mental Shield (equal to mental average)

Level 2:

  • Implant desire, opinion etc.
  • Heal mental health or condition
  • Inflict mental condition (crit. = faint)

Level 3:

  • Mind Control (dur. = mental average/2 rounds, roundup)
  • Alter Mind (4 uses per tick)
  • Clairvoyance

Mender/Medic (Physical)

Level 1:

  • Heal physical health
  • Heal physical condition
  • Corrode (dmg. = Power Effect)

Level 2:

Heal physical health can be divided between multiple targets

  • Shield (passive/active, =physical average, can be passed to other, restores after 6h sleep)
  • Inflict physical condition (crit. = faint)
  • Physical condition immunity (passive)

Level 3:

  • Regrow limbs etc.
  • Revive
  • Alter DNA (3 uses per tick)

Warrior (Physical)

Level 1:

  • Unarmed Combat + Weapon Use lvl 1
  • Overkill
  • No under 5 penalty. Roll for frenzy. Auto frenzy at 3

Level 2:

Upgraded Unarmed Combat + Weapon Use lvl 3

  • Critical
  • Called Shot (2 uses)
  • Knock-out

Level 3:

Unarmed Combat + Weapon Use lvl 5

  • Half damage (1 use). No damage (2 uses)
  • Sever limb
  • Kill (3 uses per tick)

Whisperer (Spiritual)

Level 1:

  • Forsee future
  • Curse (dur 1 round, see list of curses)
  • Speak with dead

Level 2:

Curses now last 2 rounds

  • Life Drain
  • Mass Curse
  • Reanimate Dead

Level 3:

  • Summmon Undead (lackey, dmg transference)
  • Quantum Flux
  • Whispering death (3 uses per tick)

Curse List:

  • Block sense (any 1 sense, DC 15)
  • Weakness (damage goes to 0, DC 13)
  • Paralyze (can't move at all, DC 16)
  • Sleep (DC 16)
  • Fear (Becomes Afraid, DC 14)
  • Pacify (No will to fight, DC 14)
  • Reduce Stats (any stat choice, DC 15)

Elementalist (Spiritual)

Level 1:

Most versatile power. GM decides DC and duration for everything in this power.

Choose 1 element (earth, water, air, fire). You have a basic control of the element.

Your fantasy is the limit here. For example, basic control of air can give you feather fall, or air jumps. Basic control of earth can spawn roots under your foe. With fire you can ignite anything on fire even from a distance (provided there's oxygen in the air). Water can enable you to flash freeze things or manipulate water from a distance. And anything else simple you can think of.

Level 2:

Choose 2nd element. You have medium control of both elements. You can convert one element into the other.

Level 3:

You are a true master of all elements. You can now transform air into water and freeze it above a foe, you can ignite your self in fire and fly, or use air to fly. You can freeze water inside a person and kill them instantly, or blaze them on fire. As any power, to kill someone instantly, requires 3 uses per tick. Again, DC is decided by the GM.
